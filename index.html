L.A.P. – Labyrinthine Area Puzzles


How to Play

  • Board has 4 hidden regions of 5 connected cells each.
  • Each region has a unique pattern.
  • You may probe at intersection dots to reveal which 4 patterns are present.
  • Use the sketch board to deduce the layout.
  • You have max 12 probes. When ready, submit your solution.
