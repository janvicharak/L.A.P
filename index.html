L.A.P. – Labyrinthine Area Puzzles


How to Play L.A.P. – Labyrinthine Area Puzzles

  • The board is secretly divided into 4 regions of 5 connected cells each.
  • Each region has a unique pattern (e.g., stripes, lines, waves).
  • You can probe at black dots (intersection points) to inspect a 2×2 block.
  • The probe reveals **which 4 patterns** are present, not where they are.
  • Use the sketch board to deduce the hidden map.
  • You may only use up to 12 distinct probes.
  • Click a sketch cell to assign a pattern. Click again to change it.
